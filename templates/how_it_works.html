We've all been there: you call your insurance, tax preparer, etc. for one reason or another. Depending on the business, you may end up on hold for a while. No one wants to spend their precious time sitting around until an agent is able to take their call.

This is where CallWaiting comes in.

What we do is have you tell us who you need to contact and when you can accept a call. We then take this information and forward it to companies who can then confirm those calls on a case by case basis. Both you and the agent that will take your call will be notified of this appointment. Once the time comes, the agent will call you on behalf of the company.

Easy, right?

Normally, we all dread having to call our insurance for the fifth time of the month just to be on hold for an hour resulting with minimal solutions. With this app, however, your time is not wasted and you are more likely to get the answer that you're looking for.
